## 3. Content Guidelines

### 3.1 Acceptable Content

Content uploaded to AiMediaTank must be:
- AI-generated or AI-assisted
- Original or properly licensed
- Appropriate for a general audience (unless marked otherwise)
- Compliant with all applicable laws

### 3.2 Prohibited Content

The following content is strictly prohibited:
- Child sexual abuse material (CSAM)
- Non-consensual intimate imagery
- Content promoting violence or terrorism
- Hate speech targeting protected groups
- Content infringing on intellectual property rights
- Malware or malicious code
- Spam or misleading content
- Content depicting illegal activities
- Deepfakes of real individuals without consent

### 3.3 Content Moderation

- We reserve the right to remove any content that violates these guidelines
- Repeated violations may result in account suspension or termination
- Users can report content violations through our support system

### 3.4 Age-Restricted Content

- Content intended for mature audiences must be appropriately labeled
- Users must verify their age to access age-restricted content
- Users under 18 will have automatic content filtering enabled
- Creators must properly tag content that is not suitable for all ages
- Parental controls are available for accounts used by minors

## 4. AI-Generated Content Policy

### 4.1 Definition

AI-generated content refers to media created using artificial intelligence tools including but not limited to:
- Image generators (Midjourney, DALL-E, Stable Diffusion, etc.)
- Video generators (Runway, Pika, Sora, etc.)
- Music generators (Suno, Udio, etc.)

### 4.2 Disclosure Requirements

- All content must be identified as AI-generated
- Users should specify the AI tool used when uploading content
- Misrepresenting AI content as human-created is prohibited

### 4.3 AI Tool Compliance

Users are responsible for:
- Complying with the terms of service of the AI tools they use
- Ensuring they have rights to commercialize AI-generated content
- Understanding licensing restrictions of their AI tools

### 4.4 Training Data Concerns

- Do not upload content that may have been generated using unauthorized training data
- Report content that appears to infringe on artists' or creators' rights

### 5.1 Ownership

- You retain ownership of content you create and upload
- By uploading, you grant AiMediaTank a non-exclusive license to display, distribute, and promote your content on the Platform

### 5.2 Copyright Infringement

We respect intellectual property rights and comply with the Digital Millennium Copyright Act (DMCA).

**To file a DMCA takedown notice, provide:**
- Your contact information
- Identification of the copyrighted work
- Identification of the infringing material
- A statement of good faith belief
- A statement of accuracy under penalty of perjury
- Your physical or electronic signature

Send DMCA notices to: support@aimediatank.com

### 5.3 Counter-Notification

If your content was removed and you believe it was done in error, you may submit a counter-notification with:
- Your contact information
- Identification of the removed material
- A statement under penalty of perjury
- Consent to jurisdiction
- Your physical or electronic signature

### 5.4 Repeat Infringers

Accounts with repeated copyright violations will be terminated.
